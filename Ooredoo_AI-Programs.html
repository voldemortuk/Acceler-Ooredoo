Key terms
R-C-F-T
Role, Context, Format, Tone — a 4-part structure for writing AI prompts that produce usable outputs first time, without multiple iterations.
AI Copilot
An AI assistant embedded in your workflow — like Microsoft Copilot in Teams or a custom ChatGPT. Helps with drafting, summarising, and analysing without leaving your existing tools.
Call Summarisation
AI automatically generating a structured summary of a customer call — issue, resolution, next action — reducing the time agents spend on after-call work.

Key terms
R-C-F-T
Role, Context, Format, Tone — a 4-part structure for writing AI prompts that produce usable outputs first time. The most practical skill every junior employee takes away.
APR (Accelerated Performance)
The post-programme tracking framework for junior management — measuring AI tool adoption, hours saved, and productivity improvement at 30/60/90 days after programme completion.
AI Champion
The person in a small team who adopts AI first, demonstrates it to colleagues, answers questions, and drives usage. Junior management participants are trained to become this person.
GenAI
Generative AI — AI that creates new content (text, summaries, code, emails) rather than just classifying or predicting. ChatGPT, Claude, and Microsoft Copilot are all GenAI tools.
